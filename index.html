Teisha Lou is a nonbinary voiceover artist from the United Kingdom, with five years of acting experience and equipped with a broadcast-quality home studio. They have provided voicework for Animations, Video games, Audio dramas and many more.

Their voice has been described as youthful, warm and smooth and their versatility allows them to cater to a wide variety of character types, playing ages and accents. From enchanting guardians, to your bubbly girl-next-door , they’re certain to have something in their arsenal for every project.

They studied Performing Arts Practice for Television and Film in London, but were eager to transfer everything they'd learned into voice over! Click here to view their full resume or view their demos above.

When they aren’t in the booth, they spend their time designing and coding websites (like this one!), drawing or waiting hand and foot on their fluffy bundle of chaos, Bruno.
